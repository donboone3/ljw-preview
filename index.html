About the Agency

Larry John Wright, Inc. has been a full-service advertising and marketing agency in Mesa, Arizona since 1984. We were built on the belief that great work comes from deep relationships — and four decades of results back it up.

More than 20 members of our team have been here 15 years or longer. Several client partnerships span more than three decades. That continuity is rare in this industry — and it shows in every campaign we touch.

We're tradigital by nature: TV and radio built our backbone, and digital, social, and streaming have grown alongside our clients. We don't abandon what works. We evolve it.

On-site production studio. Broadcast-quality TV, radio, and digital content — no outside vendors, no scheduling delays, no markup.
